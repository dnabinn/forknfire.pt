Our Story

Crafted for the
Curious Palate.

Fork & Fire was born from one belief: great food has no borders. We bring together the precision of Japanese sushi, the boldness of flame-grilled meats, the depth of Indian spices, and the comfort of Asian classics — all under one roof in the heart of Lisbon.

Every ingredient is sourced with intention. Every dish crafted with care. The result feels both familiar and entirely new.
